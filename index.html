About

Hi, I’m Abdulwasiu

I'm a data-driven professional with over three years of experience in data analysis, business intelligence, and database management. I specialize in transforming raw data into actionable insights using tools like Python, SQL, R, Power BI, and Looker Studio. My expertise lies in data storytelling, building insightful dashboards, and reporting solutions that support business decision-making.

Beyond analytics, I'm also a certified Data Mentor and Tutor, having trained and guided over 500 learners in data analytics and business intelligence through platforms like Udacity and Ustacky. I enjoy simplifying complex topics and delivering engaging, hands-on learning experiences using tools like Spreadsheets, Python, SQL, and Power BI. Through curriculum development and personalized mentorship, I’ve helped many aspiring analysts transition into successful data careers.

Whether it's building high-impact dashboards or mentoring the next generation of data professionals, I'm driven by the power of data to make a difference.
